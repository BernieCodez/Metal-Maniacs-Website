OUR PROGRAMS

School Visits & Demonstrations

We bring our robot to local schools to demonstrate FTC robotics and inspire students to explore STEM fields. Our interactive presentations showcase what's possible when creativity meets engineering.

  • Live robot demonstrations
  • Q&A sessions with team members
  • Interactive activities for students
  • Information about joining FTC

STEM Workshops

Our hands-on workshops introduce students to robotics fundamentals, programming, and engineering concepts. Participants get to build, code, and experiment in a fun, supportive environment.

  • Beginner robotics workshops
  • Programming basics for robots
  • Engineering design process
  • Competition strategy sessions

Mentorship Programs

We mentor younger FTC and FLL teams, sharing our knowledge and experience to help them succeed. Our team members provide guidance on robot design, programming strategies, and team management.

  • Technical mentorship for new teams
  • Strategy consultation
  • CAD design assistance
  • Programming support

Competition Support

We help organize and volunteer at local robotics competitions, providing support to teams and helping make events run smoothly. We're committed to growing the robotics community in our region.

  • Event volunteering and judging
  • Competition hosting
  • Practice field setup
  • Technical assistance at events

Community Events

We participate in community fairs, library events, and STEM nights to promote robotics and technology. These events help us connect with families and show how accessible STEM can be.

  • STEM fairs and expos
  • Library robotics nights
  • Community festivals
  • Public demonstrations

Summer Camps

We run week-long robotics summer camps where students dive deep into engineering and programming. Campers work in teams to design, build, and program their own robots to complete challenges.

  • Week-long intensive programs
  • Small team-based learning
  • Full robot build experience
  • Final showcase event

OUR IMPACT

Student Association for STEM Advocacy (SASA)

Metal Maniacs is proud to be part of the Student Association for STEM Advocacy (SASA), a national organization dedicated to promoting STEM education and advocating for increased opportunities in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics for all students. Through SASA, we work to influence policy, increase STEM funding, and ensure that all students have access to quality robotics and STEM programs regardless of their background or resources.

Speaking at World Championships

Members of Metal Maniacs have had the incredible honor of speaking at the FTC World Championships, sharing our experiences and advocating for expanded STEM opportunities on an international stage. Our team presented on the importance of outreach programs and how student-led initiatives can transform communities. Speaking at the world championships allowed us to connect with teams from around the globe, share best practices, and inspire thousands of students to become advocates for STEM education in their own communities.

NASA Space Apps Challenge

Our team participated in outreach activities during the NASA Space Apps Challenge, a global hackathon focused on space exploration and Earth science. We shared our robotics expertise with participants, demonstrated problem-solving techniques, and helped inspire innovative solutions to real-world challenges. This collaboration showcased how robotics skills translate to space technology and encouraged cross-disciplinary STEM engagement.
