WHO WE ARE

We are a dedicated middle and high school robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). Our team brings together students who love engineering, coding, and problem-solving. But above all, we're a group of friends who push each other to grow. We design, build, and program robots that compete in challenges blending creativity, precision, and teamwork.
